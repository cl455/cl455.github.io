I'm an experienced researcher and writer familiar working with both web/database and archival sources. I completed a thesis project digitally visualizing spaces of confinement with the Krakow Ghetto of Nazi-Occupied Poland as a case study. My project looks at survivor testimony as evidence, and aims to develop a possible methodology in working with LIDAR data, GIS mapping, and 3D modeling. I hope to continue to work in the realm of digital humanities. If you're curious or interested, let's chat. You can download my resume here.
