Kraków Ghetto: A Survivor's Account

Digital Mapping

For Ed Triplett's Historical ArcGIS course, I created a digital walking tour of the Kraków Ghetto based on Bernard Offen's autobiography. This page includes interactive maps, 3D views, and a timeline of historical events. The final narrative and project is presented as an ESRI Story Map.

NGA Datathon

Data Visualization

I participated as part of a team from Duke University as part of the National Gallery of Art's datathon for invited data scientists and art hsitorians to analyze, contextualize, and visualize its permanent collection data. Slides available

Main Gate of the Kraków Ghetto

3D Modeling

For my final project in a seminar jointly taught by Mark Olson and Annabel Wharton, I modeled the main gate to the ghetto on Autodesk Fusion 360 based on an archival photo.
